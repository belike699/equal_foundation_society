EQUAL FOUNDATION SOCIETY and Shelter Homes Pakistan

EQUAL FOUNDATION SOCIETY Pakistan is a private non-profit organization working for the welfare, upbringing, and education of orphan and abandoned children of Pakistan since 1977. The concept of EFS is to provide a loving, caring, and secure home to the parent-deprived community of children. This includes 15 EQUAL FOUNDATION SOCIETY, 4 EQUAL FOUNDATION SOCIETY Homes, 13 EFS Youth Homes, 22 Herman Gmeiner Schools, and 4 Technical Training Centres.

In our sponsor a child program one takes the responsibility for a child while he/she lives under our care. However, we do not give children for adoption. EQUAL FOUNDATION SOCIETY Pakistan takes pride in its invaluable work, impacting thousands of lives through the help of our donors, sponsors, and volunteers.
