What We Want to Achieve

The idea of the EFS Children's Village is an important milestone in developing a modern approach toward the welfare of children.

Deprived of their natural parents, children are placed in an environment as close to a natural home. EFS Children's Villages meet this need by providing them with loving care, security, higher education, and job training. This allows them to live normal lives, and a sense of confidence is instilled in them that develops their full potential.

The Organization aims to admit complete or social orphans children through a standard admission policy. Social orphans result from the Mother being remarried or having an incurable disease or mental illness and, consequently, being unable to provide for the children.
