Donations for charity in Pakistan

Join us in transforming the lives of vulnerable children in Pakistan. Your generous donation today will provide them with a loving home, quality education, and a brighter future. You can make your donations for the following causes:

Amount
Make an Education Endowment Rs.2,000 per month
Complete sponsorship of a child Rs.15,000 per month
Partial Sponsorship of a child Rs.5,000 per month
Complete sponsorship of a family of ten Rs.150,000 per month
Life membership of "Friends of SOS" Rs.5,000 (once only)
Donate a family house* Rs.9 million approx
Complete SOS Village for 15 Families* Rs.180-220 million
Make a donation Any amount

Construction costs vary from one region to another
